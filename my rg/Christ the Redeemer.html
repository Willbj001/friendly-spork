Christ the Redeemer

Christ The Redeemer (Portuguese: Cristo Redentor, standard Brazilian Portuguese:

[kɾistu ʁedẽtoʁ]) is an Art Deco statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, created by French-Polish sculptor Paul Landowski and built by Brazilian engineer Heitor da Silva Costa,

collaboration with French engineer Albert Caquot. Romanian sculptor Gheorghe Leonida sculpted the face.

  • Contry: Brazil

  • Year Contrution: Constructed between 1922 and 1931
