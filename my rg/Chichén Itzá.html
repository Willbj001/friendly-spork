Chichén Itzá

The Maya name "Chichen Itza" means "At the mouth of the well of the Itza." This derives from chi', meaning "mouth" or "edge" meaning "well".

Itzá is the name of an ethnic-lineage group that gained political and economic dominance of the northern peninsula.

  • Contry: Mexico

  • Year Contrution: Between 750 and 900 AD
