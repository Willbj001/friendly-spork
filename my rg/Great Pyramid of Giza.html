Great Pyramid of Giza

Great Pyramid of Giza, ancient Egyptian pyramid that is the largest of the three Pyramids of Giza

located on a rocky plateau on the west bank of the Nile River in northern Egypt.

It was built by Khufu (Cheops), the second king of Egypt's 4th dynasty and was completed about 2560 bce.
